about

I am fascinated by biological shape and by the evolutionary questions that can be addressed with its study. My research experience covers areas as varied as didactics and philosophy of science, plant defense evolution and bat taxonomy. I enjoy coding and experimenting with bioinformatic tools to address relevant questions. Passionate for transmitting knowledge. Firm believer that science should be applied in creating postive changes in the world, socially and ecologically. Interested in social justice and gender issues.
